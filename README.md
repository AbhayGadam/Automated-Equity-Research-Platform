# Automated Equity Research Platform

The **Automated Equity Research Platform** is a Python and Google Colab-based finance project that analyzes publicly traded companies through a complete equity research workflow. The platform collects market and financial data, calculates financial ratios, performs discounted cash flow valuation, compares companies with peers, measures investment risk, optimizes portfolios, and generates automated equity research reports.

This project combines finance, data analysis, software engineering, and investment research into one complete tool.

> **Disclaimer:** This project is for educational and research purposes only. It is not financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any security.

---

## Project Overview

Equity research usually requires collecting company data, reviewing financial statements, building valuation models, comparing competitors, measuring risk, and writing a final report.

This platform automates much of that workflow.

Users can enter a stock ticker, choose peer companies, adjust valuation assumptions, and generate a full company analysis through Google Colab.

Example:

```text
Ticker: NBIS
Peers: CRWV, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, ORCL
FCF Growth: 8.0%
Discount Rate: 11.0%
Terminal Growth: 2.5%
